Shirt product card

Trigger:

    Product Card:

    • Hover on
    • Hover off

    Heart Icon:

    • Hover On
    • Hover Off

    Quick Shop button:

    • Hover On
    • Hover Off

Rules:

    Product Card:

      Hover on:
      • the image will switch to the second image and reveal the heart icon, slideshow arrows, and "Quick Shop" button.
      Hover off:
      • the image will switch to the first image and hide the heart icon, slideshow arrows, and "Quick Shop" button.

    Heart Icon:

      Hover on:
      • Heart icon gray border changes to a black 1px border and hides slideshow arrows on the product card.
      Hover off:
      • Heart Icon border changes to gray and reveals slideshow arrows on the product card.

    Quick Shop Button:

      Hover on:
      • 3px Gray border appears inside the button and slideshow arrows hide on the product card.
      Hover off:
      • Hides Quick Shop Button border and reveals slideshow arrows on the product card.
